Directions

From Brighton / the South

Burgess Hill is about 10 miles north of Brighton. Leave the A23 at the A2300 junction signposted to Haywards Heath and Burgess Hill. At the first roundabout, take the third exit towards Burgess Hill. Go straight on at the second and third roundabouts. The Triangle Leisure Centre is on the Burgess Hill ringroad (the A273) - second exit on the fourth roundabout.

From London / the North

Burgess Hill is about 25 miles south of M25 junction 7 on the M23/A23. Take the M23 south towards Gatwick and Brighton. M23 becomes A23. Leave the A23 at the A2300 junction signposted to Haywards Heath and Burgess Hill. At the first roundabout, take the first exit towards Burgess Hill. Go straight on at the second roundabout. The Triangle Leisure Centre is on the Burgess Hill ringroad (the A273) - second exit on the third roundabout.

The Triangle Leisure Centre,

Parking is available in the Leisure Centre car park for competitors only.
Spectators should use town centre car parks or nearby residential roads. Please allow enough time to park and get to registration on time.
